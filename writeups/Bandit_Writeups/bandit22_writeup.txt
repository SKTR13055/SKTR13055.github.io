#Commands_Used: cron, crontab,crontab(5) (not actually used)

#Explanation:In this level it talks about the "cron" now what is cron? cron is basically a program which runs automatically at regular intervals, it contains multiple folders like cron.d, cron.daily, cron.hourly, cron.monthly, crontab, cron.weekly
these files baiscally contains instructions on the particular program are executed. To identify at what time they are executed it represents in 5 coloumns like this * * * * * where the
1st star/coloumn determines the "minute"
2nd star/coloumn determines the "hour"
3rd star/coloumn determines the "day of the month(1-31)"
4th star/coloumn determines the "month(1-12)".
5th star/coloumn determines the "day of the week(0-6,where 0 is sunday)

These star determines at what time will be executed.
