#Concept
In this level the concept involved is the Local File Inclusion(LFI).This vulnerability allows an attacker to manipulate a web application to include files from the server's filesystem,potientially exposing sensitive information.

#Analogy: The Locked Room

Imagine a house with several rooms. Each room has a specific purpose, and some rooms are locked to keep certain things private.

    Normal Access: You can enter the living room and kitchen without any issues. This is like accessing files that you are allowed to see on a website.

    Exploiting the System: Now, imagine you find a way to trick the homeowner into thinking you have permission to enter a locked room. You say, "I need to get something from the storage room," even though you shouldn't have access.

    Directory Traversal: If the homeowner believes you and lets you in, you can access whatever is in that locked room, which might include sensitive items (like passwords).
